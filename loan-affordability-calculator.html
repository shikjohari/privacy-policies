Privacy Policy

Loan Affordability Calculator App

Last Updated: January 07, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how OnlineCalcTools handles your information in connection with the Loan Affordability Calculator mobile application.

1. Privacy Commitment

The Loan Affordability Calculator is designed to help you plan your finances privately. We do not require any login, registration, or profile creation.

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect sensitive data such as your bank details, social security numbers, or addresses.
  • Local-First Data: Financial inputs like your monthly income and current EMIs are stored only on your local device. We do not have access to this information.

2. Advertising and Analytics

We use third-party services to support the app's development:

  • Google AdMob: Serves ads. May use device IDs for ad targeting.
  • Anonymized Usage: We may monitor app stability and crash reports to ensure a smooth experience.

3. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for adults planning loans and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
